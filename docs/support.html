FAQ

  • How do I unlock advanced levels? Open the app → tap any locked level (N3–N1) → tap Unlock to purchase.
  • Restore Purchases: If you already bought the unlock, tap Restore Purchases in the Unlock screen while signed in with the same Apple ID.
  • Random order: Enable random practice from the toolbar in the level screen to avoid memorizing by sequence.
  • Custom lists: Create a list, add kanji from any level, and practice only those items.
  • Privacy: We don’t collect personal data. See our Privacy Policy.
