Privacy Policy — Kanji-FlashCards

Last updated: 2025-11-11 — Bundle ID: com.barsino.kanjiflashcards

Kanji-FlashCards is an educational app to study JLPT kanji. No account is required and we do not collect personal data directly from the app.

In-App Purchase (IAP)

The app offers a non-consumable one-time purchase to unlock advanced levels (N3–N1). Payments are processed by Apple (StoreKit). We do not receive your card number or billing details. Apple may process data necessary to complete the payment and prevent fraud under Apple’s own policies.

Data we may handle

  • Purchase status: stored locally on your device to enable premium content and Restore Purchases.
  • Basic web logs: our static marketing/support pages may log standard access data (e.g., IP address, user agent) at the hosting level.

Data we do not process

  • No advertising SDKs or third-party ad networks.
  • No cross-app tracking (no ATT/IDFA prompts).
  • No profiling or data sharing for marketing purposes.

Children

The app is rated 4+ and intended for general learning. If you are a parent/guardian and have questions, please contact us.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights of access, correction or deletion. Because we do not run user accounts or store personal data on our servers, we typically do not hold information that can identify you. For payment receipts or refunds, please refer to your Apple ID account settings and Apple support.

Security

We apply reasonable measures to protect the app and associated websites.

Contact
