User Privacy Choices — Kanji-FlashCards

Last updated: 2025-11-11

Kanji-FlashCards shows no ads and uses no third-party tracking. No account is required.

In the app

  1. Restore Purchases: on the unlock screen, tap Restore Purchases to recover your one-time purchase on any device using the same Apple ID.
  2. Language: you can use the system language or (if available in the app) select a language in settings.

On iOS (system settings)

  1. Privacy: in Settings > Privacy & Security you can review system permissions. Kanji-FlashCards does not request tracking (ATT).
  2. Payments & receipts: in Settings > [your name] > Payment & Shipping you can manage payment methods and view Apple receipts.
